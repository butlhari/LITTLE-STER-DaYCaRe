Our Story

Little Stars Daycare was founded in 2016 to provide affordable, high-quality childcare for families in Johannesburg.

Our Mission

To nurture, educate, and support the growth of children in a safe and loving environment.

Our Vision

To become a leading model of daycare in South Africa by focusing on excellence in early childhood education.
